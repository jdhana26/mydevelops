How to bake classic fudgy brownies

Prep the pan and oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper. Melt the chocolate and butter: In a microwave-safe bowl, melt butter and semisweet chocolate chips together. Stir every 20 seconds until smooth, then let it cool slightly. Combine wet ingredients: Whisk sugar and eggs into the cooled butter-chocolate mixture until fully incorporated. Add dry ingredients: In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined, being careful not to overmix. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 35–40 minutes. The brownies are done when the edges are set and the center still looks slightly underbaked. Cool completely: For the best fudgy and clean-cut slices, allow the brownies to cool completely in the pan before cutting. This can take up to two hours.
